# E0 Seed Selection Strategy

## Purpose of E0 seed set
E0 establishes the initial, auditable corpus that will anchor subsequent snowballing executions.

## Why E0 prioritises precision over recall
The seed set must contain high-confidence, clearly in-scope papers to reduce propagation of off-scope records in later backward/forward snowballing. At E0, quality and traceability are prioritised over coverage breadth.

## Minimum thematic coverage expected
E0 should include coverage across the following strata:
- conceptual and theoretical papers on criminal infiltration in the legal economy;
- mafia/organised crime and legal businesses;
- criminal entrepreneurship and criminal firms;
- infiltration in public procurement and market capture;
- sectoral infiltration, including construction, waste, real estate, hospitality, logistics and other legal sectors;
- ownership, governance, corporate control and beneficial ownership;
- money laundering through legal firms, markets or corporate vehicles;
- methodological papers using judicial records, company data, network analysis, interviews or mixed methods.

## Core seed vs contextual seed
- `core_seed`: paper directly analyses criminal infiltration in legal economy contexts, mechanisms, sectors, firms, governance, or market capture.
- `contextual_seed`: paper is not centered on infiltration per se but provides essential theoretical, comparative, or methodological grounding.

## Saturation rule for E0
E0 is a setup execution and is **not** used to declare saturation.

## Traceability rule
Every seed entry must include a transparent, explicit reason for inclusion and a source basis so that inclusion decisions are auditable.
